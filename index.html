WORLD WAR 1?

Worl War 1 was the first mechanized war in history. In the beginning, fighting was simular to wars fought in the previous century. But new and terryfying weapons were introdused that completely changed the whole style of warfare. Aircraft were used for the first time to observe the enemy and to locate suitable targets for the long-range artillery. Later on, fighter planes began to shoot down the spotters, introdusing aerial warfare. Aircraft and Zepplein airships were used as bombers. The most terrifying new weapon was poison gas, which was used by both sides. It caused millions of deaths and terrible suffering. Tanks also made their first appearance.

FACT FILE

The Worl War 1 (1914-18) brought the first appearance of armoured tanks in battle. They were able to break through enemy lines and create openings for troops to advance through. Earlier use of tanks could have saved lives and helped shorten the war
